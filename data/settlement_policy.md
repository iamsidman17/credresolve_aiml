
# CredResolve Settlement Policy v2025

## 1. Personal Loans (PL)
* **Standard Case**: Max discount is 20% of Principal Outstanding.
* **High Delinquency (DPD > 90)**: Max discount is 35%.
* **Critical Condition (Job Loss)**: If the borrower explicitly mentions 'Job Loss' or 'Unemployment', max discount is 50%.
* **Medical Emergency**: If 'Hospital' or 'Medical' is mentioned, max discount is 45%.

## 2. Credit Cards (CC)
* **Standard Case**: Max discount is 30%.
* **High Delinquency (DPD > 90)**: Max discount is 50%.
* **Small Balances (< 50k)**: Flat 10% discount only, regardless of DPD.

## 3. General Rules
* All offers must be rounded to the nearest 100.
* Never accept an offer lower than the calculated limit.
